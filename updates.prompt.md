
## Dropfall scores and replays

we want to keep track of how many games each player has won in the current set of games, like street fighter does. 

We also want to record a reply of the game for the players to watch and optionally save, and also to use at the end of a round by showing a replay of the loser falling from the level.

## Player creation

We want players to be able to pick from a big set of colours on the game setup screen, where the user(s) enter their names there should be a preview of their ball, and they should be able to toggle between colours and change hats and see the live preview of their ball

In single player the player should see the AI ball there and be able to change its colour and hat
